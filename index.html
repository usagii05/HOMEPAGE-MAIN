Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal

    A root canal is a dental procedure to repair a severely decayed or infected tooth. During the procedure, the dentist removes the damaged part inside the tooth, cleans it, and seals it to prevent further infection. It helps save the tooth and relieves pain.

  • Alignment Teeth

    Teeth alignment is a procedure to straighten crooked or misaligned teeth using braces or clear aligners. It improves appearance, makes cleaning easier, and reduces dental problems.

  • Cosmetic Teeth

    Cosmetic teeth procedures improve the appearance of teeth. They include treatments like whitening, veneers, bonding, and crowns, fixing issues like discoloration, chips, gaps, and alignment.

  • Oral Hygiene

    Oral hygiene procedures involve brushing teeth, flossing, and using mouthwash regularly to keep the mouth clean and healthy. Regular dental check-ups and cleanings are also important for maintaining oral health.

  • Tooth Removal

    Teeth removal, or tooth extraction, is a straightforward dental procedure where a dentist removes a tooth from its socket in the jawbone.

  • Cavity Inspection

    Cavity inspection is when a dentist checks teeth for decay. They use a mirror and tool to look for soft spots or discoloration on the tooth surface and may take X-rays if needed. If cavities are found, the dentist suggests treatment options like fillings to fix them.
